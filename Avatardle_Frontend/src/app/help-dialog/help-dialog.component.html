Objective

Guess the character based on different characteristics! Resets daily at 12:00 AM UTC.

Color Indicators

The colors indicate whether the guessed character matches the traits of the target character.

Incorrect - when the character traits don't match.
Partially correct - only applicable to the affiliations category, when only some affiliations may match.
Correct - when the character traits do match.

Categories

Gender

A character's gender can be male, female, or none.

Nationality

This refers to where a character originates. Sometimes the place of origin may not be a clearly defined nation (like the spirit world), in that case the nationality would be marked as none.

Bending Element

This only includes the 4 basic bending elements: Water , Earth , Fire , and Air .

Affiliations

This refers to more specific qualities, traits, and groups related to the character. A maximum of 3 affiliations are shown per character. If a character has more than 3 affiliations in their pool, affiliations will be chosen at random.

Objective

Guess who said the quote! Resets daily at 12:00 AM UTC.

Hints

There are 3 hints in total, each unlocked after a certain number of guesses.

Next Line - the immediate next line that is spoken.
Previous Line - the immediate previous line that is spoken.
Episode Name - the episode in which the quote is spoken.

What if the quote is the first/last line of an episode?

The hints will state whether the quote is the first or last line.

Image Effects

The image starts off zoomed in and in grayscale. Each guess reduces the effects on the image.
